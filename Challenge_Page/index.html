What Kind of Art Unlocks Joy?

The kind of art you were taught was good was "good art." Or maybe even "skillful art."
I'm going to disagree with that. Good art is art that brings joy, or does not harm others.
It can be non-sensical. It can be silly. It can be poorly done. It can be all of these things and still bring joy.
Art can be self expression. It can be simply an act of play not yet shamed out of our lives and duties as adults.

The question one asks when seeing the above should never be: "But did this make me money."
It should be, "Did I enjoy the process and did it make life a little more worth living?"
